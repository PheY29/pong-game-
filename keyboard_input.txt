1) choose your game_mode : up/down arrow to choose then press ENTER
2) choose your VS (AI or two player) : left/right arrow to choose then press ENTER
	2.1) you can ESCAPE if you failed the game mode selection
3) during a game press ESCAPE to pause the game : left/right arrow to choose then press ENTER


one player :
	left player : up = z
		      down = s
	right player : AI

two player :
	left player : up = z
		      down = s
	right player : up = up arrow
		       down = down arrow
